About

My name is Daniel Josley. I was born in Tuba City Arizona and I am Lokaa Dine (Reed People), Born for Toahani (Near The Water), My maternal grandfather is Tobaahi (Edge Water) and My paternal grandfather is Ta'chiin'nii (Red Running into Water). Growing up I was always artistic, it came naturally to me.
I never thought itwould become my passion. I have been painting for 3 years now and I absolutely love what I do. I absolutely enjoy creating my own works of art. It started out as a simple painting of my grandfather. As I started to experiment more and more, that simple painting turned into my very own master pieces. I like to make my art unique, a one of a kind. I am a self-taught artist. Each painting I create is unique in its own way. With each painting, I have created my own technique in which I am known for. Words of wisdom that I take in every time I pick up a brush. Every mistake is an eye opener and a new direction. Respect the brush and everything will follow. Tomorrow is the beginning of the rest of my life. Respect your elders and teach with passion.

Work

I am a member of The Art of the People. They have embraced me and helped me with being known and promoting me as Artist of the year for Shonto's Rock the Canyon 2019. The Painted Desert Project, where I got the chance to work with Mural Artists from all over, who taught me the steps to outdoor creations. I am an Assistant to Shonto Begay on his Art camping trips with NAU. I will hold these experiences dearly and always remember them.
